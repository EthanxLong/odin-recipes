50 Cent Hotdogs

About a dollar per serving!

Ingredients

  • Hot dog bun - $0.88

Steps

  1. Wrap hotdog frank in paper towel, then microwave for 40-50 seconds
  2. Put hotdog on bun
  3. Add condiments
