Easy Fried Rice

super cheap

Ingredients

  • Old Cold Rice - NA
  • Soy Sauce - $1.8
  • Eggs
  • Frozen Vegetables - $1.94
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Oil pan, make sure its hot
  2. fry eggs and when its cooked, put aside
  3. fry vegetables, put aside
  4. put cold rice in a pan, then fry it while adding soy sauce, eggs and vegetables.
  5. mix well, and add soy sauce till you like it

Very filling! Add chicken for a delicious meal
