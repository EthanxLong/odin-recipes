Simple Spaghetti and Meatballs

About a dollar per serving!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles - $1.28
  • Spaghetti Sauce - $1.88
  • Frozen meatballs - $4.52

Steps

  1. Boil spaghetti till cooked (around 8-12 minutes)
  2. While spaghetti is boiling, put spaghetti sauce and frozen meatballs in pan, and cook
  3. When spaghetti is cooked, combine the sauce and noodles and heat for 2 mins

This takes around 15 minutes to make
